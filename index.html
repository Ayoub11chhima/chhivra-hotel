A Century of Excellence

True to its singular identity, Chhivra is constantly reinventing itself, fashioning a unique magic woven of elegance and exception.

From the moment you arrive, you will be captivated by the harmony of the surroundings, the opulence of the materials and the excellence of the craftsmanship revealed in each and every detail.

Wherever you look, you will be struck by a majestic beauty — the softness of velvet and leather, the contours of sculpted plaster and zellige tilework, the freshness of cool marble.
